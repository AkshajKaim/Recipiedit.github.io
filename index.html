Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Black Pepper

Instructions:

Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water. In a separate pan, cook bacon until crispy. Whisk eggs and cheese in a bowl. Combine spaghetti, bacon, and egg mixture. Stir and serve with freshly ground black pepper.

Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Fettucine Pasta
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic

Instructions:

Season chicken with salt and pepper, cook until golden. Prepare fettucine pasta. In a separate pan, heat cream and add garlic, then combine with chicken, pasta, and cheese for a creamy dish.
